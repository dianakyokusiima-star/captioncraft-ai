Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026 · Amiisukyok Creatives
What we collect
We collect only the minimum to operate the service. Your product descriptions are sent directly to the Anthropic Claude API to generate your copy. Inputs are not stored after your session ends. License keys are verified locally inside the extension — no personal data is sent to any server.
How your data is used
Inputs are processed solely to generate your requested copy. We do not sell, share, or rent your data. Plan tier and daily usage are stored locally using Chrome's storage.sync — never sent to our servers.
API key security
The Anthropic API key is embedded in the extension's background service worker and is used solely to call the Claude API on your behalf. All AI generation happens via a direct encrypted connection to Anthropic — no third-party proxy or backend is involved.
Payments
Payments are processed by Payhip. We never see or store your card details. Your billing information is governed by Payhip's privacy policy.
Your rights
You can clear all local data at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing Chrome's extension storage. To request account deletion, email privacy@amiisukyokcreatives.com.
